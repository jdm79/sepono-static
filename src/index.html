About Us

Sepono Properties is based in Bethnal Green, close to a lot of the properties we look after. We are proud to charge less than the larger agencies.

Whole apartments to rent
We currently have whole apartments in Shoreditch for rent. These apartments are already fully furnished. Bills are not included in the rental price.
Rooms in Flatshares
We also have rooms available across east London in flatshares for those on a smaller budget. All rooms come fully furnished, with wifi and other bills (gas, elec, water, council tax) included.
Information for Landlords
If you are a landlord and would like to rent out your apartment, please get in touch. Sepono Properties has over five years experience looking after properties across London, and are happy to charge less than larger agencies for this service. Contact us for more information.
